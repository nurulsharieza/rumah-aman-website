What We Provide

📚 Education

We believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Children at Rumah Aman receive proper schooling, tuition support, and learning tools to help them succeed.

❤️ Care & Support

Our dedicated caretakers provide emotional support and guidance, helping each child feel loved, valued, and empowered to pursue their dreams.

🏠 Safe Shelter

We offer a clean, safe, and nurturing living environment — a true home where children can grow in dignity, security, and happiness.
